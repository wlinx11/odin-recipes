Pasta al peso

Home

An exquisite pasta that you're gonna love.

Here in this recipe we will guide you along the preparation of this special pasta

Ingredients

  • Homemade pesto
  • Pasta
  • Sea salt
  • Pasta cooking water
  • Parmigiano Reggiano

Steps

  1. You can make the simple pesto while the pasta cooks. It's so easy to make! Gather up the ingredients and get the pasta water salted and boiling.
  2. Make the quick pesto and set aside while you cook your pasta to al dente.
  3. Strain the pasta, reserving some of the starchy pasta water, and add to a bowl. Add in the pesto sauce, cheeses, and stir to combine. Add in some of the pasta water to loosen the sauce. Check seasoning, add salt, if needed.
  4. Serve immediately. If you like, grate on some Parmigiano Reggiano or a really good pecorino.
  5. Serve and BUON APPETITO! Enjoy!
